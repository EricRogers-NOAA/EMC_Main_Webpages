181  . mbit/8
182  . + nbyt
183  . + 4

187 C COMPRESSED DATA INTO SECTION 4).

189  nbyt = mbit/8
190 
191 C NOW, STORE THE TOTAL LENGTH OF THE BUFR MESSAGE (IN SECTION 0).
192 
193  mbit = 32
